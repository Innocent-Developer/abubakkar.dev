Do you work with international clients remotely?

Yes - I work remotely with clients worldwide from Lahore, Pakistan (UTC+5), with professional English and experience across time zones. Typical reply time is within 24 hours.

What services do you offer?

Backend and API development (Node.js, FastAPI), full SaaS product builds, WhatsApp Business API chatbots with AI, React/Next.js frontends, and Linux server/DevOps work.

Can you build a WhatsApp chatbot for my business?

Yes - I build WhatsApp Business API chatbots with AI auto-replies for e-commerce, real estate and lead capture, including ForiSay, a live WhatsApp AI platform.

Both, depending on scope. Well-defined projects work best as fixed price with milestones; ongoing or evolving work suits hourly or a monthly retainer. Every project starts with a free scoping call.

A backend API or chatbot MVP typically ships in 2–4 weeks; a full SaaS product in 6–10 weeks depending on scope. You get a clear milestone plan before work starts.
